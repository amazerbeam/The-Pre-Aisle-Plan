# Value Betting Guide

## The Core Concept

A value bet exists when:
**Your estimated probability > Implied probability from odds**

This is the ONLY way to profit long-term. Picking winners isn't enough — you must pick winners at odds that underestimate their chances.

## Converting Odds to Probability

**Decimal odds → Implied probability:**
```
Implied % = (1 / Decimal Odds) × 100

Examples:
- Odds 2.00 → 50% implied
- Odds 1.50 → 66.7% implied
- Odds 3.00 → 33.3% implied
- Odds 4.00 → 25% implied
```

## Finding Edge

**Edge = Your estimate - Implied probability**

| Your Estimate | Odds (Implied) | Edge | Action |
|---------------|----------------|------|--------|
| 55% | 2.00 (50%) | +5% | BET |
| 45% | 2.00 (50%) | -5% | SKIP |
| 35% | 3.50 (28.6%) | +6.4% | BET |
| 60% | 1.40 (71.4%) | -11.4% | SKIP |

## Estimating True Probability

Factors to weight (in rough order of importance):

1. **Recent form** (last 5 matches) — 30%
2. **Home/away splits** — 20%
3. **Head-to-head** — 15%
4. **Squad strength/injuries** — 20%
5. **Motivation/context** — 15%

## Common Value Spots in EPL

- **Underdogs at home** — Public underrates home advantage
- **Draws** — Most bettors avoid draws; often underpriced
- **Under goals** — Public loves goals; overs often overpriced
- **Teams after bad result** — Overreaction creates value
- **Early season** — Odds based on last season; slow to adjust

## Red Flags (False Value)

- Odds moved against you (sharps already took value)
- Key player injury not yet priced in (value will disappear)
- Too good to be true (you're missing something)
