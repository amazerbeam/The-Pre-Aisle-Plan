# Question Framework

Use these questions to gather context before answering nutrition queries.

## Required Context (Ask 2-4)

### Goal-Based
- "What's your primary goal with this?" (weight, energy, health condition, curiosity)
- "Are you trying to solve a specific problem or learn generally?"

### Personal Context
- "Any relevant health conditions I should consider?"
- "Any allergies or foods you avoid?"
- "What's your current activity level?"

### Practical Constraints
- "Are you following any specific eating pattern?"
- "Any budget or time constraints?"

### Specificity Check
- "Do you want general guidance or something tailored to your situation?"

## Question Selection by Topic

| Topic | Priority Questions |
|-------|-------------------|
| Weight | Goal direction, current approach, timeline expectations |
| Energy | Sleep, meal timing, activity level |
| Supplements | Current diet, specific deficiency concerns, medications |
| Diet comparison | Goals, lifestyle fit, sustainability concerns |
| Food myths | Source of claim, specific concern, context |
| Condition-related | Diagnosis status, current management, healthcare team |

## Skip Questions When

- User provides detailed context upfront
- Question is purely factual (e.g., "What foods contain vitamin C?")
- Follow-up to previous conversation with context established
- User explicitly requests quick answer

## Question Phrasing

- Keep questions conversational, not clinical
- Combine related questions to reduce back-and-forth
- Explain why you're asking if not obvious
- Offer to skip if user prefers general answer
