## Weight Loss Phase

When ready to lose weight:

### Calorie Target
- **Daily:** ~1,900 cal
- **Weekly:** ~13,300 cal
- **Expected loss:** ~0.5 lb per week

### Variant Strategy
Switch from Full variants to: **Mostly Light + one Standard per day**

### Run Day Strategy
On run days, can eat ~2,200 cal:
- 2,200 (food) - 300 (run) = 1,900 net

## Diet Breaks

| Amount to Lose | Approach |
|----------------|----------|
| Small (1/2 - 1 stone) | Straight through, no breaks needed |
| Medium (1 - 1 1/2 stone) | 6-8 weeks deficit, then 1 week maintenance, repeat |
| Large (1 1/2+ stone) | 4-6 weeks deficit, then 1-2 weeks maintenance, repeat |

### Signs You Need a Break
- Constantly hungry despite eating enough
- Energy crashed
- Sleep problems
- Irritable / mood tanked
- Workouts suffering

---

## Measuring Progress

### Scale (Weekly Average Method)
1. Weigh daily, same conditions (morning, after bathroom, before eating)
2. Record all 7 numbers
3. Calculate the weekly average
4. Compare weekly averages, not individual days

**Don't use:** Single weigh-ins or week's lowest (misleading due to water fluctuation)

### Tape Measure
Measure every 1-2 weeks:
- **Waist (at navel)** - most important for belly fat
- Hips
- Chest

## Key Principles

1. **Weekly average matters, not any single day**
2. **Can't spot-reduce belly fat** - it comes off last, keep losing overall
3. **Same foods, different portions** - Light/Standard/Full variants for different goals
4. **Listen to your body** - take breaks if struggling
5. **Alcohol calories count** - budget them in, don't ignore them
