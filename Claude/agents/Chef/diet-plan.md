## What NOT To Do

- No skipping protein to save calories
- No extra cheat meals on Saturday/Sunday
- No "making up" for Friday by starving Saturday
- No running 4x per week without carbs (injury risk)
- No whole bottle of wine / 6 Guinness (old pattern)

---

## Troubleshooting

**Not losing weight after 3 weeks?**
1. Verify tracking accuracy (weigh food)
2. Check for hidden calories (cooking oils, sauces)
3. Confirm weekend eating is actually normal
4. Reduce target to 1,750 cal if needed

**Always hungry?**
1. Increase protein (up to 180g)
2. Add more vegetables for volume
3. Check sleep quality (affects hunger hormones)
4. Ensure you're eating enough at meals (not grazing)

**Hit a plateau?**
1. Verify tracking hasn't slipped
2. Take a diet break (1 week at 2,400 cal)
3. Then resume at 1,900 cal
